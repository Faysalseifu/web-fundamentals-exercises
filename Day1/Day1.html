Platform capabilities

GPS radius discovery

Availability calendar

How it works

A clear journey from listing to handoff.

1

List or search

Lessor creates a listing with photos, location, pricing, and availability. Lessees discover nearby items in seconds.

2

Reserve and verify

The booking calendar checks conflicts while KYC and trust visibility help each party make informed decisions.

3

Pay and communicate

Funds move through escrow, deposits are held securely, and chat stays open for time-sensitive coordination.
